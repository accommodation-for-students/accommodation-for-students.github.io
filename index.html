Junior Front-End Developer

A little about us

The home of student accommodation, www.accommodationforstudents.com was launched in 2000. Since launch AFS has grown to be the leading search engine for student accommodation. Our simple aim is to enable students to search and find the accommodation that most closely matches their requirements, whether that is a house, flat, hall of residence or spare room. We pride ourselves on having the widest range of accommodation possible, ensuring a constant stream of high quality student tenants for our partners. Our rich history and heritage in this sector means that we have unique access to data and insight. In 2018, after months of planning, we embarked on a major upgrade of AFS, to ensure that we remain the leading search engine for student accommodation.

What you'll do

Hi, we're AFS! We're looking for a front-end developer at the start of their software development journey to join us on our quest to build awesome things for students. We're looking for someone with a passion for all things front-end to join our existing team of 4. We began the process of migrating the old AFS website over to our new platform last year and still have a number of pages and user journeys to rebuild. We're using React on the front-end and .NET Core on the backend. With that in mind we're ideally looking for someone who is passionate about React and has good knowledge of CSS. Also we're a responsive site and build mobile first so it'd be beneficial if you have knowledge of mobile first design. Further, we value keeping AFS fast across all devices and compliant with accessibility standards (a11y) and would love for you to eventually dive deeper into these areas.

We have tons of great work in the pipeline and we believe our environment and iterative approach make it easy to deliver great quality work, while learning and improving.
